## Overview & User Guide

This seven-tab empirical protocol tracks the progressive neutralization of the four fiat mechanisms (fractional-reserve lending, Cantillon effects, K-shaped divergence, and buy-borrow-die incentives) as Bitcoin adoption advances. The four tests (A.1–A.4) directly evaluate the structural obsolescence of Keynesian demand-management tools.

**The Four Tests and Their Correspondence to the Trilogy**  
- **A.1 Praxeological Test** → Section 1 of all three papers  
- **A.2 Transmission-Mechanism Metrics** → Section 2 of the Dismantlement Framework & Capstone  
- **A.3 Historical Episode Review** → Section 3 of the Capstone  
- **A.4 Forward Metric Tracking** → Section 3 of the Dismantlement Framework & Section 5 of the Capstone  

**How to Update (Quarterly Cadence)**  
1. Mark row “In Progress” in the Schedule tab.  
2. Update Tabs 3–6 with latest data.  
3. Dashboard and Realignment Index update automatically.  
4. Mark row “Complete” and add notes.
